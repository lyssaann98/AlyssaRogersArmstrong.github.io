About Me

My name is Alyssa Rogers-Armstrong and I am currently a 7th grade mathematics teacher. After graduating from University of Pittsburgh during the middle of the Covid-19 pandemic, I was accepted into the prestigious corps program, Teach for America, which has an acceptance rate of 11-15%. Teach for America is a nonprofit organization who chooses promising leaders to grow and strengthen the movement for educational equity in underserved communities. I was placed in the D.C metro area and have been teaching in Alexandria City Public Schools in Virginia for the past two years./center>
Throughout my time in the corps, I have re-found my love for mathematics and further developed my passion for data analytics. I started out by completing a Data Analytics Certification Course from Google, and used those skills to analyze my classroom student data to improve test scores and student engagement./center>
While being a full-time teacher, I am constantly learning new skills and refining the ones I have already learned to better prepare me for an entry level data analyst position in the near future./center>
